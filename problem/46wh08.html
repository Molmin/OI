题目描述

充满快乐的，Hyunuk 将要举办一个关于将来的一年有多伟大的大会！

大会有 nnn 个讲座。Hyunuk 有两个可供选择的会场 A 和 B。对于 nnn 个讲座中的每一个，演讲者选择了两个时间区间 [sai,eai][sa_{i},ea_{i}][sa​i​​,ea​i​​]（sai≤eaisa_{i} \leq ea_{i}sa​i​​≤ea​i​​）和 [sbi,ebi][sb_{i},eb_{i}][sb​i​​,eb​i​​]（sbi≤ebisb_{i} \leq eb_{i}sb​i​​≤eb​i​​）。如果大会在 A 会场举办，那么讲座就会在 saisa_{i}sa​i​​ 到 eaiea_{i}ea​i​​ 的时间举行；如果大会在 B 会场举行，那么该讲座就会在 sbisb_{i}sb​i​​ 到 ebieb_{i}eb​i​​ 的时间举行。Hyunuk 只能选定两个会场中的一个，然后所有讲座都要在那个会场举行。

两个讲座被称为冲突，当且仅当它们共用了同一个时间点。正式地，我们称一个在区间 [x,y][x,y][x,y] 中举办的讲座和一个在 [u,v][u,v][u,v] 区间举办的讲座冲突，当且仅当 max(x,u)≤min(y,v)\max(x,u) \leq \min(y,v)max(x,u)≤min(y,v)。

我们称一个听众可以参加所有讲座的一个子集 sss，当且仅当这个子集中任何一对讲座都不冲突。注意：是否能参加这个子集 sss 的可能取决于 Hyunuk 选择的是 A 会场或是 B 会场来举办大会。

对于一个子集 sss，若在一个会场，观众可以参加，而在另一个会场，观众却不可以参加，那么它被称为“会场敏感的”。

对于观众来说，是否存在一个会场敏感的子集 sss 是一个重要的问题，因为观众无法确定讲座时间是否会冲突。Hyunuk 会开心当且仅当不存在任意一个会场敏感的子集。请判断 Hyunuk 是否会开心。

Filled with optimism, Hyunuk will host a conference about how great this new year will be!

The conference will have nnn lectures. Hyunuk has two candidate venues aaa and bbb . For each of the nnn lectures, the speaker specified two time intervals [sai,eai][sa_i, ea_i][sa​i​​,ea​i​​] ( sai≤eaisa_i \le ea_isa​i​​≤ea​i​​ ) and [sbi,ebi][sb_i, eb_i][sb​i​​,eb​i​​] ( sbi≤ebisb_i \le eb_isb​i​​≤eb​i​​ ). If the conference is situated in venue aaa , the lecture will be held from saisa_isa​i​​ to eaiea_iea​i​​ , and if the conference is situated in venue bbb , the lecture will be held from sbisb_isb​i​​ to ebieb_ieb​i​​ . Hyunuk will choose one of these venues and all lectures will be held at that venue.

Two lectures are said to overlap if they share any point in time in common. Formally, a lecture held in interval [x,y][x, y][x,y] overlaps with a lecture held in interval [u,v][u, v][u,v] if and only if max(x,u)≤min(y,v)\max(x, u) \le \min(y, v)max(x,u)≤min(y,v) .

We say that a participant can attend a subset sss of the lectures if the lectures in sss do not pairwise overlap (i.e. no two lectures overlap). Note that the possibility of attending may depend on whether Hyunuk selected venue aaa or venue bbb to hold the conference.

A subset of lectures sss is said to be venue-sensitive if, for one of the venues, the participant can attend sss , but for the other venue, the participant cannot attend sss .

A venue-sensitive set is problematic for a participant who is interested in attending the lectures in sss because the participant cannot be sure whether the lecture times will overlap. Hyunuk will be happy if and only if there are no venue-sensitive sets. Determine whether Hyunuk will be happy.
