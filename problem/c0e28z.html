There are nnn bags, each bag contains mmm balls with numbers from 111 to mmm . For every i∈[1,m]i \in [1, m]i∈[1,m] , there is exactly one ball with number iii in each bag.

You have to take exactly one ball from each bag (all bags are different, so, for example, taking the ball 111 from the first bag and the ball 222 from the second bag is not the same as taking the ball 222 from the first bag and the ball 111 from the second bag). After that, you calculate the number of balls with odd numbers among the ones you have taken. Let the number of these balls be FFF .

Your task is to calculate the sum of FkF^kF​k​​ over all possible ways to take nnn balls, one from each bag.
