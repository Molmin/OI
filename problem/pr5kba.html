Tony Stark is playing a game with his suits (they have auto-pilot now). He lives in Malibu. Malibu has nnn junctions numbered from 111 to nnn, connected with n−1n-1n−1 roads. One can get from a junction to any other junction using these roads (graph of Malibu forms a tree).

Tony has mmm suits. There's a special plan for each suit. The iii-th suit will appear at the moment of time tit_{i}t​i​​ in the junction viv_{i}v​i​​, and will move to junction uiu_{i}u​i​​ using the shortest path between viv_{i}v​i​​ and uiu_{i}u​i​​ with the speed cic_{i}c​i​​ roads per second (passing a junctions takes no time), and vanishing immediately when arriving at uiu_{i}u​i​​ (if it reaches uiu_{i}u​i​​ in time qqq , it's available there at moment qqq , but not in further moments). Also, suits move continuously (for example if vi≠uiv_{i}\neq u_{i}v​i​​≠u​i​​, at time ti+12cit_i+\dfrac 1{2c_i}t​i​​+​2c​i​​​​1​​ it's in the middle of a road. Please note that if vi=uiv_{i}=u_{i}v​i​​=u​i​​ it means the suit will be at junction number viv_{i}v​i​​ only at moment tit_{i}t​i​​ and then it vanishes.

An explosion happens if at any moment of time two suits share the same exact location (it may be in a junction or somewhere on a road; while appearing, vanishing or moving).

Your task is to tell Tony the moment of the the first explosion (if there will be any).
